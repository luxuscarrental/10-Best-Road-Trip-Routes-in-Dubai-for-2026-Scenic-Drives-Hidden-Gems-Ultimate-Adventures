The 10 Best Road Trip Routes

1. Jebel Jais – The Ultimate Mountain Drive (Top Pick)

Distance: ~190 km round trip | Time: 2.5–4 hours

Drive to the UAE’s highest peak (1,934m) via the thrilling, winding Jebel Jais Road. The route features dramatic hairpin bends, stunning Hajar Mountain views, and the world’s longest zip line at Jais Adventure Zone.

Highlights in 2026: New viewing platforms, upgraded rest areas, and cooler mountain weather. Stop for coffee at the summit café and enjoy panoramic vistas.

Best for: Adventure seekers and photography enthusiasts.

2. Hatta – Dubai’s Mountain Escape

Distance: ~130 km one way | Time: 1.5–2 hours

Head east through scenic valleys to Hatta, an oasis town with turquoise lakes, hiking trails, and heritage villages. The drive transitions from desert plains to lush green mountains.

Highlights: Hatta Dam, rock climbing, kayaking, and the new eco-lodges. Perfect for a peaceful nature break.

Pro Tip: Combine with a stop at Al Hajar Mountains viewpoints.

3. Liwa Oasis & Empty Quarter (Rub’ al Khali)

Distance: ~320–350 km one way | Time: 3–4 hours

Venture deep into Abu Dhabi’s desert for golden dunes, towering sand mountains, and peaceful oases. The drive along smooth highways gradually gives way to raw desert beauty.

Highlights 2026: More luxury desert camps, hot air balloon rides, and stargazing opportunities. Visit Qasr Al Sarab Resort for ultimate luxury.

Best for: Desert lovers and photographers.

4. Fujairah East Coast via Mountains

Distance: ~280–300 km round trip | Time: 3–5 hours

Cross the Hajar Mountains on scenic winding roads to reach Fujairah’s pristine Gulf of Oman beaches.

Highlights: Snoopy Island for snorkeling, Dibba Rock, and coastal drives with dramatic cliffs. Stop at Masafi for fresh mountain water and dates.

Best for: Beach + mountain combo seekers.

5. Jebel Hafeet – Abu Dhabi’s Iconic Winding Road

Distance: ~300 km round trip from Dubai | Time: 3–4 hours

One of the most thrilling drives in the UAE, featuring over 60 curves leading to Jebel Hafeet’s summit with sweeping desert views.

Highlights: Green Mubazzarah hot springs at the base and the dramatic mountain road itself.

Best for: Driving enthusiasts.

6. Al Qudra Lakes & Desert Loop

Distance: 60–100 km round trip | Time: 1–2 hours

A short yet stunning escape into the desert just outside Dubai.

Highlights: Man-made lakes, cycling tracks, flamingos, and peaceful sunset spots. Ideal for families and beginners. 2026 Update: Expanded recreational areas and new glamping options.

7. Al Ain – The Garden City Oasis

Distance: ~140 km one way | Time: 1.5–2 hours

Drive through desert highways to Al Ain, a UNESCO World Heritage site known for its lush date palm oases and historic forts.

Highlights: Jebel Hafeet (if combining routes), Al Ain Zoo, and traditional souks.

Best for: Culture and history lovers.

8. Ras Al Khaimah (RAK) Coastal & Mountain Route

Distance: ~170 km one way | Time: 1.5–2.5 hours

Head north for a mix of beach resorts, mountains, and adventure parks.

Highlights: Jais Mountain (extension), beaches, and the new luxury developments in 2026.

Best for: Luxury and adventure mix.

9. Sharjah & East Coast Heritage Drive

Distance: 100–200 km round trip | Time: 2–4 hours

Explore cultural Sharjah, then continue to Kalba or Khorfakkan for mangrove forests and beaches.

Highlights: Sharjah’s museums, historic districts, and scenic coastal roads.

Best for: Cultural road trips.

10. Sheikh Zayed Road to Abu Dhabi Corniche & Yas Island

Distance: ~280 km round trip | Time: 3–5 hours

A modern classic combining city lights, desert views, and futuristic attractions.

Highlights: Yas Island (Ferrari World, Warner Bros.), Saadiyat Island beaches, and Abu Dhabi’s stunning Corniche.

Best for: First-time visitors and family trips.

Essential Road Trip Tips for Dubai in 2026

  • Best Time: November to March for pleasant temperatures.
  • Vehicle: Prefer a 4x4 or SUV for desert and mountain routes.
  • Fuel & Tolls: Use Salik tags and fill up before long drives. Apps like Google Maps or UAE’s RTA app are reliable.
  • Driving Rules: Strict speed enforcement, zero tolerance for distracted driving, and mandatory seatbelts. Carry water, snacks, and a charged phone.
  • Car Rental: Book from reputable providers like Sixt, Hertz, or local companies for flexible options.

Safety & Sustainability

Follow speed limits, stay hydrated, and respect local customs. Support eco-friendly operators and avoid off-road driving unless in designated areas.
